Beyond Borealis

The Borealis Garden Initiative

Synopsis

An expedition to the far north. A bunker buried beneath the ice. A project designed to preserve life.

Selected to collaborate with the Borealis Garden Initiative, a team of scientists and specialists travels to a remote Arctic station dedicated to safeguarding plant life for the future of humanity.

The mission seems routine. Document, evaluate, and return.

But upon arrival, nothing is quite as expected.

Equipment out of place. Deteriorating facilities. Incomplete records. Silences that shouldn't exist.

As isolation deepens, the expedition begins to face a series of unsettling events that defy logical explanation.

What started as a scientific mission soon becomes a struggle to understand what happened at Borealis… and whether they are truly alone.
