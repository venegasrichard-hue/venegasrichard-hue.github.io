Richard V. Corwin

Richard V. Corwin writes psychological thrillers focused on memory, identity, and hidden pasts. His stories explore ordinary lives interrupted by a single event, where truth becomes uncertain and every character hides something.

His work focuses on tension, atmosphere, and psychological conflict, creating slow-burn suspense and unsettling revelations.
